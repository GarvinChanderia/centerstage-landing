For marketing copy, we can represent corn, milk, lotus, and soybean-based fabrics under one broader phrase:

**Natural-Origin Fibers**

This is generic enough to cover all these sources without sounding too technical or making strong sustainability claims.

Other options we can consider:

• **Nature-Derived Fibers**
Softer and more lifestyle-led.

• **Natural-Origin Comfort Fibers**
Best if we want to connect it directly with comfort.

• **Bio-Based Comfort Fibers**
More modern and innovation-led.

• **Botanical & Protein-Based Fibers**
More technical, but accurate because some fibers are plant-based and some are protein-based.

Recommended wording:

**Natural-Origin Fibers**
Thoughtfully selected from nature-derived sources for a softer hand feel, breathable comfort, and an elevated everyday experience.
